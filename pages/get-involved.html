Get Involved

Join our church family and make a difference in God's kingdom

Use Your Gifts to Serve God

God has given each of us unique gifts and talents to serve Him and bless others. At Salem African Church of Christ, we believe that every member has a role to play in building God's kingdom. Whether you're new to faith or have been walking with God for years, there's a place for you to serve and grow.

"As each has received a gift, use it to serve one another, as good stewards of God's varied grace."
- 1 Peter 4:10

Ministry Opportunities

Choir Ministry

Lead worship through music and song, helping to create an atmosphere of praise and worship that draws hearts closer to God.

  • Sunday worship leading
  • Special event performances
  • Thursday & Friday rehearsals
Requirements: Love for music, commitment to rehearsals, heart for worship

Ushering Team

Welcome and assist church members and visitors, ensuring everyone feels at home and comfortable during services.

  • Greeting and welcoming
  • Seating assistance
  • Collection assistance
Requirements: Friendly personality, punctuality, servant's heart

Children's Ministry

Teach and mentor the next generation, helping children grow in their knowledge and love of God through engaging activities.

  • Sunday school teaching
  • Children's activities
  • Special programs
Requirements: Love for children, patience, teaching ability

Technical Team

Handle sound, lighting, and media equipment to ensure smooth and professional church services and events.

  • Sound system operation
  • Equipment maintenance
  • Event support
Requirements: Technical aptitude, reliability, willingness to learn

Cleaning & Maintenance

Keep God's house clean and well-maintained, ensuring a welcoming environment for worship and fellowship.

  • Church cleaning
  • Basic maintenance
  • Grounds keeping
Requirements: Physical ability, attention to detail, team spirit

Evangelism Team

Share the Gospel in the community, reaching out to those who need to hear about God's love and salvation.

  • Community outreach
  • Door-to-door evangelism
  • Special campaigns
Requirements: Passion for souls, boldness, good communication skills

  • Special Prayers: We pray for God's blessings on your new year
  • Birthday Wishes: Receive warm wishes from your church family
  • Community Connection: Strengthen bonds within our church community

Feedback & Suggestions

Share Your Feedback

Your feedback helps us improve our ministry and better serve our church family. Please feel free to share your thoughts, suggestions, or concerns.

Feedback Guidelines

We Value Your Input

Your feedback is important to us and helps us grow as a church community. Here are some guidelines:

  • Be Respectful: Please communicate with love and respect
  • Be Specific: Detailed feedback helps us understand and address issues better
  • Be Constructive: Suggest solutions when possible
  • Be Patient: We will review and respond to all feedback

Response Time

We aim to respond to all feedback within 7 business days. For urgent matters, please contact the church office directly.
