About Our Church

Discover our history, mission, and the heart behind Salem African Church of Christ

Welcome to Salem African Church of Christ

Salem African Church of Christ, Abekoko Parish, is a vibrant Christian community located in the heart of Agege, Lagos. We are more than just a church; we are a family united by faith, hope, and love in Jesus Christ.

Our church stands as a beacon of hope in the community, offering spiritual guidance, fellowship, and practical support to all who seek to grow in their relationship with God. We believe in the power of community and the importance of walking together in faith.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurture spiritual growth, and build a strong Christian community that serves God and impacts our neighborhood positively. We are committed to:

  • Proclaiming the Gospel with boldness and love
  • Fostering spiritual growth and discipleship
  • Serving our community with compassion
  • Building strong Christian fellowship

Our Vision

To be a thriving, Christ-centered church that transforms lives and communities through the power of God's love, creating a lasting impact for His kingdom. We envision:

  • A church known for its love and unity
  • Expanding God's kingdom locally and globally
  • Transforming lives through Christ's love
  • Being a light in our community

What We Believe

Our faith is built on the solid foundation of God's Word. These core beliefs guide our worship, fellowship, and service to God and our community.

The Holy Scripture

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and our ultimate authority for faith and practice.

Jesus Christ

We believe in Jesus Christ as our Lord and Savior, fully God and fully man, who died for our sins and rose again.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the Holy Spirit as our Comforter, Guide, and the source of spiritual gifts and power for Christian living.

Salvation by Grace

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ, not by works, and is available to all who believe.

The Church

We believe the church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, discipleship, and service.

Second Coming

We believe in the personal return of Jesus Christ and the resurrection of the dead unto eternal life.

Our Core Values

Love

Love is at the center of everything we do. We strive to love God with all our heart, soul, and mind, and to love our neighbors as ourselves, creating a welcoming environment for all.

Faith

Our faith in Jesus Christ is the foundation of our church. We trust in God's promises, rely on His strength, and live by faith in all circumstances.

Hope

We are a people of hope, anchored in the promises of God. Our hope in Christ gives us strength to face challenges and inspires us to share this hope with others.

Unity

We value unity in diversity, coming together as one body in Christ despite our different backgrounds, working together for God's kingdom.

Service

Following Christ's example, we are committed to serving others with humility and compassion, both within our church family and in the broader community.

Integrity

We strive to live with integrity, being honest, transparent, and faithful in all our dealings, reflecting Christ's character in our words and actions.

Our History

A Legacy of Faith

Salem African Church of Christ, Abekoko Parish, has been a cornerstone of faith in the Agege community for many years. Our church was established with a vision to create a place where people could come together to worship God, grow in faith, and support one another.

From our humble beginnings, we have grown into a vibrant community of believers who are passionate about serving God and making a positive impact in our neighborhood. Our church has weathered many seasons, always remaining faithful to our calling to be a light in the community.

Today, we continue to build on the strong foundation laid by those who came before us, always looking forward to what God has in store for our future. We are committed to preserving our rich heritage while embracing new opportunities to serve and grow.

Our Journey Continues

As we look to the future, we remain committed to our mission of spreading God's love, building strong relationships, and making a lasting impact in our community. We invite you to be part of our continuing story.
