Our Church Leadership

Meet the dedicated servants who lead our church family with wisdom, integrity, and love

Servant Leadership

Our church is blessed with dedicated leaders who serve with humility, wisdom, and a heart for God's people. Each member of our leadership team brings unique gifts and experiences to guide our church family in spiritual growth, community service, and faithful stewardship.

"But whoever wants to become great among you must be your servant, and whoever wants to be first must be slave of all."
- Mark 10:43-44

Church Leadership

Our dedicated leadership team works together in unity to serve God and guide our church family. Each leader brings unique gifts and experiences to ensure effective ministry and spiritual growth.

VICAR IN CHARGE
Rev'd Canon Olufemi Oyelola
Vicar in Charge

Provides spiritual leadership and pastoral care to our church family. His dedication to God's word and compassionate heart for the people makes him a beloved shepherd of our congregation.

PEOPLE'S WARDEN
Bro. Olawale Rotimi
The People's Warden

Serves as a vital link between the congregation and church leadership, ensuring the spiritual welfare of our church family and representing the voice of the people in church governance.

PASTOR'S WARDEN
Bro. Seun Igbinyade
Pastor's Warden

Works closely with church leadership to ensure smooth operations and effective ministry, supporting pastoral duties and coordinating church activities with dedication and organizational excellence.

CHURCH TREASURER
Bro. Dotun Osoja
Church Treasurer

Manages church financial resources with wisdom, integrity, and transparency, ensuring proper stewardship of God's resources and maintaining accurate financial records.

FINANCIAL SECRETARY
Bro. Kehinde Adegbola
Financial Secretary

Works closely with the Treasurer to maintain accurate financial records and assist in day-to-day financial operations, ensuring proper documentation and transparency in all transactions.

CHURCH AUDITOR
Bro. Femi Adeleye
Church Auditor

Ensures transparency and accountability in all financial matters through independent oversight, reviewing financial records and maintaining the trust and confidence of our congregation.

CHIEF VESTRY CLERK
Bro. Seun Tanimonure
Chief Vestry Clerk

Maintains accurate records of church meetings, decisions, and important documents with meticulous attention to detail, preserving our church's history and facilitating effective communication.

Our Leadership Principles

Servant Leadership

Following Christ's example, our leaders serve with humility and love, putting the needs of the congregation and community first. We believe that true leadership comes through serving others.

Integrity & Transparency

We maintain the highest standards of integrity in all our dealings, ensuring transparency in decision-making and financial stewardship. Trust is the foundation of effective leadership.

Collaborative Ministry

Our leadership team works together in unity, combining diverse gifts and perspectives to guide the church effectively. We believe in shared responsibility and mutual accountability.

Biblical Foundation

All our leadership decisions are grounded in biblical principles and guided by prayer. We seek God's wisdom in all matters concerning the church and its ministry.

Growth & Development

We are committed to the spiritual growth and development of our congregation, providing opportunities for learning, service, and personal spiritual advancement.

Community Impact

Our leadership extends beyond the church walls to impact our community positively, showing Christ's love through practical service and outreach programs.

Connect with Our Leadership

Our church leaders are here to serve you. If you have questions, concerns, or would like to discuss any church matters, please don't hesitate to reach out.

Our Leadership Principles

Servant Leadership

Our leaders follow the example of Jesus Christ, leading through service, humility, and love. We believe that true leadership comes from serving others and putting the needs of our church family first.

  • Leading by example
  • Serving with humility
  • Caring for the flock

Biblical Foundation

  • Scripture-based decisions
  • Prayer-guided leadership
  • Spirit-led ministry
