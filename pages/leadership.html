Our Leadership Principles

Servant Leadership

Our leaders follow the example of Jesus Christ, leading through service, humility, and love. We believe that true leadership comes from serving others and putting the needs of our church family first.

  • Leading by example
  • Serving with humility
  • Caring for the flock

Biblical Foundation

All our leadership decisions and actions are grounded in biblical principles and guided by the Holy Spirit. We seek God's wisdom in all matters concerning our church family and community.

  • Scripture-based decisions
  • Prayer-guided leadership
  • Spirit-led ministry
