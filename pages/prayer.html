Prayer Points for the Week

Weekly Prayer Focus

Join us in prayer as we seek God's face together as a church family. These prayer points guide our weekly intercession.

Monday - Leadership Wisdom

Pray for wisdom and guidance for our church leadership team. Ask God to grant them discernment in making decisions that will benefit our church family and advance His kingdom.

Tuesday - Healing & Health

Intercede for the sick and those in need of healing. Pray for God's healing touch upon our members who are facing health challenges, and for comfort for their families.

Wednesday - Unity & Love

Pray for unity and love within our church family. Ask God to help us love one another as Christ loves us, and to maintain the bond of peace in our fellowship.

Thursday - Financial Breakthrough

Pray for financial breakthrough for our church members and for the church's building projects. Ask God to provide for every need according to His riches in glory.

Friday - Evangelism & Outreach

Pray for our evangelism efforts and community outreach programs. Ask God to open doors for sharing the Gospel and to prepare hearts to receive His love.

Saturday - Youth & Children

Pray for our youth and children, that they may grow in the knowledge and fear of the Lord. Ask God to protect them from negative influences and guide their steps.

Sunday - Thanksgiving & Worship

Give thanks to God for His faithfulness and goodness. Pray for our worship service, that hearts will be prepared to receive God's Word and that His presence will be manifest among us.

Food for Thought

Daily Inspiration

"This is the day the Lord has made; let us rejoice and be glad in it."
- Psalm 118:24

Every day is a gift from God, wrapped in His love and filled with His possibilities. When we wake up each morning, we have the choice to see the day as God sees it - as an opportunity for joy, growth, and blessing.

The psalmist reminds us that this day - today - is not an accident. It's not just another day on the calendar. It's a day that the Lord has specifically made for us. He has prepared it with purpose, filled it with potential, and given it to us as a gift.

Let us approach each day with gratitude, joy, and expectation of His goodness. No matter what challenges we may face, we can rejoice because we know that God is with us, and He has good plans for our lives.

Reflection Questions:

  • How can I show gratitude for this day that God has made?
  • What opportunities for joy can I find today?
  • How can I be a blessing to someone else today?

How to Pray Effectively

Biblical Principles for Prayer

1. Pray with Faith

"And whatever you ask in prayer, you will receive, if you have faith." - Matthew 21:22

Believe that God hears and will answer according to His will.

2. Pray According to God's Will

"And this is the confidence that we have toward him, that if we ask anything according to his will he hears us." - 1 John 5:14

Align your prayers with God's purposes and desires.

3. Pray with Persistence

"And he told them a parable to the effect that they ought always to pray and not lose heart." - Luke 18:1

Don't give up; continue praying until you receive an answer.

4. Pray with Thanksgiving

"Do not be anxious about anything, but in everything by prayer and supplication with thanksgiving let your requests be made known to God." - Philippians 4:6

Always include gratitude in your prayers.
