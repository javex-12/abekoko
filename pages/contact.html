Frequently Asked Questions

Visiting Our Church

What should I expect on my first visit?

You can expect a warm welcome, uplifting worship, and practical Bible teaching. Our services last about 2 hours and include praise, worship, prayer, and a sermon.

What should I wear?

Come as you are! We welcome people in both casual and formal attire. What matters most is your heart, not your clothing.

Is parking available?

Yes, parking is available around the church premises. Our ushers will help direct you to available spots.

Church Membership

How do I become a member?

Contact us to learn about our membership process. We offer classes to help you understand our beliefs and get connected with our church family.

Are children welcome?

Absolutely! Children are always welcome in our services. We have programs specifically designed for different age groups.

How can I get involved?

There are many ways to get involved! Visit our Get Involved page to learn about volunteer opportunities and ministries.

Emergency Contact
