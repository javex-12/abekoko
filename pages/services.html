What to Expect

First Time Visiting?

We're excited to welcome you to our church family! Here's what you can expect when you visit us.

Warm Welcome

Our ushers and greeters will welcome you with genuine warmth and help you feel at home.

Dress Code

Come as you are! We welcome people in casual or formal attire - what matters is your heart.

Service Length

Our services typically last about 2 hours, including worship, teaching, and fellowship time.

Children Welcome

Children are always welcome in our services. We have programs specifically designed for young ones.

Parking

Parking is available around the church premises. Our ushers can help direct you to available spots.

Fellowship

Stay after service for fellowship time where you can meet other members and enjoy refreshments.
