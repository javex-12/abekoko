Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ and build a strong Christian community rooted in biblical teachings, love, and fellowship. We are committed to nurturing spiritual growth, providing pastoral care, and serving our community in Abekoko Parish, Agege Lagos.

  • Proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ
  • Build strong Christian community
  • Provide pastoral care and support
  • Serve our local community

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and love in Abekoko Parish and beyond, where every person can experience God's transforming love, grow in faith, and discover their purpose in Christ. We envision a church family that reflects the diversity and unity of God's kingdom.

"Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you."
- Matthew 28:19-20

Our Core Beliefs

Scripture

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God, our ultimate authority for faith and practice. Through Scripture, God reveals His will and guides our lives.

Salvation

We believe in salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ. His death and resurrection provide forgiveness of sins and eternal life for all who believe.

Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. Each person is fully God, yet there is one God.

Church

We believe the church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, discipleship, ministry, and evangelism in our community and beyond.

Service

We believe in serving others as Christ served us, showing love through practical acts of kindness, compassion, and justice in our community.

Second Coming

We believe in the personal return of Jesus Christ and the resurrection of the dead, leading to eternal life for believers and judgment for all.

Our History

Our Heritage

Salem African Church of Christ has been a cornerstone of faith in the Abekoko community for many years. Our church is part of the African Church movement, which has deep roots in Nigerian Christianity and has been instrumental in spreading the Gospel throughout West Africa.

Located in the heart of Abekoko Parish, Agege Lagos, our church has grown from a small gathering of believers to a thriving community that serves families throughout the area. We are proud of our heritage and committed to continuing the legacy of faith, hope, and love that has been passed down through generations.
