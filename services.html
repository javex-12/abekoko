What to Expect

Service Duration

Our Sunday service typically lasts 2-3 hours, including:

  • 30-45 minutes of praise and worship
  • 15-20 minutes of prayer time
  • 45-60 minutes of preaching
  • Fellowship and announcements

Dress Code

We welcome you to come as you are! However, we encourage modest and respectful attire:

  • Business casual or formal wear
  • Traditional African attire welcome
  • Modest and respectful clothing
  • Special colors for special occasions

First Time Visitors

If you're visiting for the first time, please don't hesitate to introduce yourself to our ushers or pastor. We'd love to welcome you personally and answer any questions you might have.
