Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 27, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Salem African Church of Christ ("we," "our," or "us") collects the following information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and address when you submit forms
  • Prayer Requests: Information you provide in prayer request forms
  • Testimonies: Content you share in testimony submissions
  • Volunteer Information: Details about your skills and availability
  • Website Usage: Basic analytics about how you use our website

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and prayer requests
  • Provide spiritual guidance and pastoral care
  • Coordinate volunteer activities
  • Send church updates and announcements (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and services

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only:

  • With church leadership for pastoral care purposes
  • When required by law
  • With your explicit consent

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of communications

6. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools to improve user experience and understand website usage patterns.

7. Third-Party Services

We use EmailJS for form submissions and may use other third-party services. These services have their own privacy policies.

8. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
