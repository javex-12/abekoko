Register Your Birthday

Let us celebrate your special day with you! Register your birthday so we can remember and celebrate together as one church family.

Why Register Your Birthday?

  • Church Family Celebration: We celebrate together as one big family
  • Special Prayers: We pray for God's blessings on your new year
  • Birthday Wishes: Receive warm wishes from your church family
  • Community Connection: Strengthen bonds within our church community
  • Thanksgiving: Give thanks to God for another year of life

Feedback & Suggestions

Share Your Feedback

Your feedback helps us improve our ministry and better serve our church family. Please feel free to share your thoughts, suggestions, or concerns.

Feedback Guidelines

We Value Your Input

Your feedback is important to us and helps us grow as a church community. Here are some guidelines:

  • Be Respectful: Please communicate with love and respect
  • Be Specific: Detailed feedback helps us understand and address issues better
  • Be Constructive: Suggest solutions when possible
  • Be Patient: We will review and respond to all feedback

Response Time

We aim to respond to all feedback within 7 business days. For urgent matters, please contact the church office directly.

Confidentiality

All feedback is treated confidentially. Anonymous submissions are completely private.
